# Lab 3 — Advantis Dental Surgeries (ADS)

## Functional requirements (short statements)

1. The system shall allow an **Office Manager** to **register Dentists** who apply to join the network, assigning each Dentist a **unique ID** and recording **First Name**, **Last Name**, **Contact Phone Number**, **Email**, and **Specialization**.
2. The system shall allow an **Office Manager** to **enroll Patients**, recording **First Name**, **Last Name**, **Contact Phone Number**, **Email**, **Mailing Address**, and **Date of Birth**.
3. The system shall allow a **Patient** to **request an appointment** by **phone** (call-in).
4. The system shall allow a **Patient** to **request an appointment** by submitting an **online form** on the ADS website.
5. The system shall allow an **Office Manager** to **book** an appointment after receiving an appointment request.
6. When an appointment is booked, the system shall **send a confirmation email** to the **Patient** and shall **record** the appointment in the system.
7. Each **Appointment** shall be scheduled for a **specific date and time** and shall be associated with **one Dentist**, **one Patient**, and **one Surgery** location where the visit occurs.
8. The system shall maintain **Surgery** information for each location, including **name**, **location address**, and **telephone number**.
9. The system shall allow a **Dentist** to **sign in** and view a **list of all Appointments** assigned to that Dentist, including **details of the Patients** scheduled to be seen.
10. The system shall allow a **Patient** to **sign in** and view **their Appointments**, including **information about the Dentist** they are booked to see.
11. The system shall allow a **Patient** to **request cancellation** of an appointment.
12. The system shall allow a **Patient** to **request a change** (reschedule) of an appointment.
13. The system shall **enforce** that a **Dentist** cannot have **more than five (5) Appointments in any single calendar week** (including proposed new bookings).
14. The system shall **prevent** a **Patient** from requesting a **new appointment** if that Patient has an **outstanding, unpaid bill** for dental services already received.
15. The system shall **track billing** for dental services in a form sufficient to determine whether a Patient has an **outstanding unpaid balance** that blocks new appointment requests.
